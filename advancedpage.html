These film equipment images are just some of few that can help enhance your quality of filmmaking. Having these pieces of equipment handy is essential for any top-tier filmmaker. Although these are more expensive pieces of equipment out there, these are the next steps from beginnner to becoming more advanced.

Shotgun Mic:

This mic can be placed onto the boom pole that can reach into scenes.

Shoulder Rig:

These allow you to move with the camera without being obnoxiously bumpy if you were to simply hold the camera. For example, if you were to run in an action scene with the camera on this, it would look smoother.

Dolly Tripod:

This allows for smooth tracking shots, like a zoom in form far away for example.
